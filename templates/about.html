Our mission

According to a belief, one who dies in Kashi attains moksha. Many elderly people either come or are abandoned in the last stage of their life in the city. They canâ€™t work for their living and mostly depend on daan received from tourists. They were the worst hit during the COVID 19 phase when tourism sector had collapsed.

Our vision

There are many unorganised ashrams and sarais that accommodate such people, which mostly depend on funds raised from the local community and tourists. Our aim is to provide these people with something more than mere donations. We aim to connect these Ashrams and Rest Houses with prospective donors.

Our value

We also aim to provide these people with a source of employment suitable for their age. Through our Monitoring and Feedback system, we will analyse the whereabouts of the ashrams before adding them. Any donor can know the complete amount raised for the work from us and we plan to follow a model similar to RTI.
